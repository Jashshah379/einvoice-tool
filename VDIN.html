Click "Validate" or "Generate & download JSON" to see the output here.
What to do with this JSON: upload it on the official e-Invoice portal at einvoice1.gst.gov.in under e-Invoice → Bulk Upload (or through your GSP/accounting software if you use one). The portal validates it, returns a signed IRN + QR code, and that's what you attach to the invoice copy you give the buyer — this tool only prepares the JSON, it doesn't talk to the government portal directly. If your turnover requires it, invoices must be uploaded within 30 days of the invoice date.
Runs entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded anywhere. Company profiles & draft data are stored locally in this browser only. Schema: e-Invoice Version 1.1 (IRP).
